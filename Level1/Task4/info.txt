Two Types of Resource Controls
1. Requests (Minimum Guarantee)

"I need at least this much to run"
Like reserving a seat on a bus

yamlresources:
  requests:
    memory: "256Mi"  # I need at least 256 MB
    cpu: "250m"      # I need at least 0.25 CPU
Translation: "Give me at least 256 MB RAM and 0.25 CPU, or don't start me at all"

2. Limits (Maximum Allowed)

"I cannot use more than this much"
Like a speed limit on a highway

yamlresources:
  limits:
    memory: "512Mi"  # I can't use more than 512 MB
    cpu: "500m"      # I can't use more than 0.5 CPU
Translation: "Even if I want more, stop me at 512 MB RAM and 0.5 CPU"

Complete Example  :

apiVersion: v1
kind: Pod
metadata:
  name: my-app
spec:
  containers:
    - name: app
      image: nginx
      resources:
        requests:           # What I need minimum
          memory: "128Mi"   # Need at least 128 MB
          cpu: "100m"       # Need at least 0.1 CPU
        limits:             # What I can't exceed
          memory: "256Mi"   # Can't use more than 256 MB
          cpu: "200m"       # Can't use more than 0.2 CPU
